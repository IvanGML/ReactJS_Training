How to use

To use this application, you need to take a few simple steps.

  1. First of all you have to decide whether your conversion ratio will be static (i.e. length, weight, etc.) or variable (for example currency).
  2. The next step is to select a dimension or enter conversion rate in the field "rate"
  3. Fill input fields to make a measurement
  4. Have a nice day!
